About Aaron

Aaron Arnstein is a junior at the University of Maryland. He is the station director of the school’s student-run radio station, WMUC Sports. Arnstein oversees more than 30 students in the writing, broadcasting and podcasting departments. He is also a broadcaster and writer for Maryland baseball’s flagship station, the Maryland Baseball Network. In addition, Arnstein is a broadcaster for the Big Ten conference’s streaming platform, Big Ten Plus. Aaron grew up in New Jersey and currently resides in Voorhees, New Jersey.
